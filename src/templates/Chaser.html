Toyota Chaser (year 96-01)

 

This is the Toyota Chaser, which is a long rear wheel drive car, highly popular in the drift community due to its long wheelbase. It has the 1JZ engine which is the older generation to what is in the supra, but is again highly tunable capable of rediculous power. The standard engine is a 160bhp 2.5 litre. These are again a rare car due to their popularity, however many japanese locals are still driving them around as the same with the other cars mensioned on this web app as daily cars not knowing that they are sitting on goldmines if they were to export the car and put it through export auctions.
