Honda Integra DC2 (year 96-00)

 

The Integra Type R DC2 is an iconic japanese classic, known to be one of the best handling front wheel drive cars of all time. It weighs merely 1140kg, and has a 187bhp 1.8 VTEC engine, although the engine size and output seems relatively low, the car has a very impressive power to weight ratio.
