Nissan S15 (year 99-02)

 

The Nissan Silvia S15, is a latter model of the s13 silvia covered in the previous page, it is two generations newer, although has the same fundamentals, it is made slightly more heavy, durable, and less tin-can like inside. It also boasts a 2 litre 247 bhp engine, which again is turbo charged and highly tunable. These cars are fairly rare due to the high demand coming from japan, meaning they are selling for a lot of money.
