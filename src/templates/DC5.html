Honda Integra DC5 (year 01-06)

 

This is the Integra Type R DC5, another front wheel drive iconic japanese car. This one is the newer model to the DC2, and is slightly more refined to drive, this however means the car is slightly heavier, and to accomodate that they have put in a more powerful 2 litre engine which puts out around 220bhp. Although most enthusiasts prefer the DC2, this newer model is still a classic and well respected vehicle.
