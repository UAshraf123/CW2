Nissan S13 (year 89-95)

 

This is the Nissan Silvia S13, which is starting to become an extremely rare car, as this is a very popular drift car (going sideways) amongst drift enthusaists. The car is very well balanced and there is a rediculous amount of modifications on the market for these vehicles. the car has a 2.4 litre turbo charged engine which produces around 160bhp, but is highly tunable to achieve more impressive numbers.
